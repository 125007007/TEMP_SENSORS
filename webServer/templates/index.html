Humidity Information

At 100% relative humidity, the air is saturated and is at its dew point. Iron will rust when the air climbs over 50% relative humidity. Steel will rust when the relative humidity is over 80%. As long as the temperature of the item you want to prevent getting condensation is over the abiant temperature, no condensation will form.
